### The "Abio" Platform System
**Technology Stack:**
- **Primary Platform:** "Abio" handles both main event AND sales lobbies
- **Architecture:** Main lobby with breakout room mini-lobbies
- **Load Balancing:** 500 people max per room, opens new rooms as needed
- **Switchboard Operator:** Routes 1,200+ people into appropriate breakouts

**Staffing Structure:**
- **Per Lobby:** 1 host + 2 chat moderators  
- **Bot Integration:** Trained bots handle FAQ + low-hanging fruit questions
- **Human Oversight:** Humans vet bot responses for quality control
- **Scalability:** Different salespeople cap at different levels (20-100 people based on ability)

### Why Breakout Rooms Work
**Psychology:** 1,200 people in one room = "my question will never get answered"
**Solution:** Smaller rooms feel intimate, people can't tell total audience size
**Result:** Higher engagement and conversion in mini-lobby environment

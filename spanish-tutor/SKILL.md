## Quick Start

### Daily Routine (15-20 minutes)
1. **Morning lesson** - New vocabulary via voice note
2. **Practice session** - Use new words in context  
3. **Evening review** - Spaced repetition of previous lessons

### Lesson Structure
Each lesson includes:
- **3-5 new words/phrases** with pronunciation
- **Cultural context** - when and how to use them
- **Practice challenge** - real-world application
- **Next-day review** - spaced repetition reinforcement

## Learning Methodology

Based on proven language acquisition principles:
- **Comprehensible Input** (Krashen) - 80% familiar + 20% new
- **Task-Based Learning** - Real scenarios over abstract grammar
- **Spaced Repetition** - Scientific review intervals
- **Cultural Immersion** - Context over translation

## Lesson Categories

### Foundation (Months 1-2)
- Essential greetings and daily interactions
- Family and personal vocabulary
- Present tense mastery
- Numbers, time, and basic questions

### Building (Months 3-5)  
- Business terminology and introductions
- Past and future tenses
- Travel and dining vocabulary
- Email and phone communication

### Fluency (Months 6-8)
- Complex grammar structures
- Industry-specific vocabulary (wellness, education, technology)
- Presentation and meeting language
- Negotiation and persuasion phrases

### Mastery (Months 9-12)
- Advanced business conversations
- Cultural nuances and regional differences
- Public speaking and presentations
- Native-level idiomatic expressions

## SLIDE 4: L.O.F.T.Y. FRAMEWORK (10 minutes)
*Energy: Systematic, empowering*

### Framework Introduction
**"L.O.F.T.Y. isn't just a cute acronym - it's your quality control system. Every question you craft needs to pass these five tests, or it won't work."**

### Teaching Each Letter (Spend 2 minutes on each)
**L - Lifting:** 
- "If the question doesn't make you feel more empowered, it fails the test."
- Example: "Why am I so stuck?" ❌ vs "Why am I becoming so free?" ✅

**O - Ownership:**
- "No victim language allowed. You must take responsibility for your reality."
- Example: "Why do people treat me badly?" ❌ vs "Why do I attract such respectful people?" ✅

**F - Future-focused:**
- "Questions about the past keep you trapped there."
- Example: "Why did this happen to me?" ❌ vs "Why am I becoming unstoppable?" ✅

**T - Truthful:**
- "Must align with your deepest values, or your subconscious will reject it."
- Example: Check - does this question represent who you truly want to be?

**Y - Yes-inducing:**
- "If it doesn't make you excited to ask it, it won't work."
- Example: Ask the question and feel your energy - does it lift or drain?

### Energy Cues
- **Use the failed examples first** - they'll relate
- **Then show the transformation** - create the "aha" moment
- **Get nods and agreement** - check for understanding

### Transition
"Now let's put this into practice. We're going to do some transformation work together."

## SLIDE 5: THE QUESTION AUDIT (8 minutes)
*Energy: Introspective but safe*

### Exercise Setup
**"This is a judgment-free zone. We all have unconscious patterns - that's why we're here. Be honest with yourself because that's where the power is."**

### Instructions (Clear and Specific)
1. **"Write down 3 questions you commonly ask yourself. Don't think too hard - what does that voice in your head usually say?"**
   *(Give 2 minutes)*

2. **"Now, next to each question, write 'E' for empowering or 'D' for disempowering."**
   *(Give 1 minute)*

3. **"Finally, I want you to actually ASK yourself each question internally and notice how it makes you FEEL in your body."**
   *(Give 2 minutes)*

### Facilitation Tips
- **Walk around the room** - offer encouragement, not judgment
- **Share your own example** if people seem stuck: "I used to ask 'Why am I behind everyone else?'"
- **Normalize the disempowering ones** - "If you have mostly D's, you're normal and we're about to fix it"

### Debrief
**"Raise your hand if you discovered you've been asking mostly disempowering questions."**
*(Validate their honesty)*
**"Congratulations - awareness is the first step to transformation."**

### Transition
"Now let's take those disempowering questions and flip them into something that serves your highest self."

## SLIDE 6: "THE FLIP" EXERCISE (12 minutes)
*Energy: Collaborative transformation*

### Partner Setup
**"Find someone near you - preferably someone you don't know well. We're about to get vulnerable in the most empowering way."**

### Exercise Instructions
**"Partner A - Share your biggest frustration or challenge. Don't go into a long story - just name it clearly."**
*(Give 2 minutes)*

**"Partner B - Listen for the unconscious question they're asking. Usually it starts with 'Why can't I...' or 'Why don't I...' or 'Why am I so...'"**
*(Give 1 minute to identify)*

**"Now together - transform that anti-lofty question into a lofty one using our framework. Test it - does it pass L.O.F.T.Y.?"**
*(Give 4 minutes)*

**"Switch roles and repeat."**
*(Another 5 minutes)*

### Facilitation During Exercise
- **Move through the room** - offer guidance where needed
- **Give examples** when people are stuck:
  - "Why can't I lose weight?" → "Why does my body naturally gravify toward its perfect weight?"
  - "Why don't I have enough money?" → "Why am I such a magnet for abundance?"
- **Celebrate breakthroughs** - acknowledge transformations you overhear

### Energy Management
- **Keep it moving** - don't let people get stuck in their stories
- **Focus on the question transformation** - not therapy
- **Create safety** - "There are no wrong answers, only learning"

"Now I'm going to give you a comprehensive toolkit of lofty questions for every area of your life."

## TROUBLESHOOTING GUIDE

### If Energy Drops
- **Ask a direct question:** "Who's feeling this shift in energy?"
- **Get them moving:** "Everyone stand up and stretch"
- **Share a personal story** of transformation
- **Use humor** to reset the room

### If Someone Is Skeptical
- **Acknowledge their perspective:** "I get it - this might sound too simple"
- **Focus on the science:** "Let's just look at the neuroscience for a moment"
- **Invite experimentation:** "Try it for one week and see what happens"
- **Don't convince** - let them opt out with dignity

### If Someone Gets Emotional
- **Normalize it:** "Transformation can bring up feelings - that's normal"
- **Create safety:** "Take your time - we're here with you"
- **Redirect to hope:** "What you're feeling is the old pattern releasing"
- **Offer private support** if needed

### If Exercise Stalls
- **Give specific examples** instead of waiting for volunteers
- **Pair people yourself** if they're not partnering
- **Share your own vulnerability** to model openness
- **Adjust timing** - don't rush breakthroughs

### Common Questions & Responses

**Q: "What if I don't believe the lofty question?"**  
A: "You don't need to believe it - you just need to be curious about it. Curiosity is enough to activate your RAS."

**Q: "How is this different from affirmations?"**  
A: "Affirmations tell your brain what to believe. Questions tell your brain what to look for. Your brain naturally resists beliefs but loves to answer questions."

**Q: "What if nothing happens after 30 days?"**  
A: "Then you've learned that your old pattern of questioning wasn't serving you, and you've practiced redirecting your attention. Both are valuable skills."

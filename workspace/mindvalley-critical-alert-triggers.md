# Mindvalley Critical Alert System

## **Instant Manual Triggers:**

### "Check Mindvalley NOW" 
Runs immediate comprehensive scan when you need urgent intel

### "Critical Alert Search"
Emergency-focused search for crisis situations, threats, viral negative content

### "Competitive Watch"
Check what competitors are doing/saying about Mindvalley

## **Auto-Alert Triggers:**
The daily system will IMMEDIATELY send a dedicated alert message (not just include in daily report) when:

- **New 1-star Trustpilot review** (within 24h)
- **Reddit post about Mindvalley with >50 upvotes** (negative)
- **News article mentioning Mindvalley** (any sentiment)
- **Social media viral post** (>1000 interactions)
- **Competitor attack/comparison** gaining traction
- **App store rating drop** below 4.0
- **Customer service issue trending** on social

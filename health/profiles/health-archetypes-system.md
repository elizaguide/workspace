## Progression Logic

### Primary Factors (Weighted)
1. **Overall Health Score** (40% weight)
   - Calculated from biomarker optimization
   - Supplement adherence
   - Lifestyle factors

2. **Goal Achievement** (25% weight)
   - Progress toward specific health targets
   - Consistency in healthy behaviors
   - Milestone completions

3. **Optimization Level** (20% weight)
   - Number of biomarkers in optimal range
   - Advanced protocols implemented
   - Biohacking sophistication

4. **Time & Consistency** (15% weight)
   - Length of sustained healthy behaviors
   - Streak maintenance
   - Long-term commitment

### Age-Adjusted Expectations
- **20-30**: Focus on building foundation, preventing future issues
- **30-45**: Optimization and performance enhancement
- **45-60**: Maintenance and age-defying strategies
- **60+**: Longevity and vitality preservation

## Implementation Features

### Profile Integration
- Current archetype displayed prominently

### Social Elements
- Share archetype achievements
- Compare with friends (motivational, not competitive)
- Mentorship connections between levels

### Rewards System
- Archetype-specific perks and content
- Exclusive supplement recommendations by level
- Advanced features unlock with progression
- Celebration animations for level-ups

## Motivational Framework

### Core Principles
1. **Always Forward-Moving**: Even setbacks are reframed as learning
2. **Personal Growth**: Focus on individual journey, not comparison
3. **Heroic Narrative**: Each person is the hero of their health story
4. **Celebration**: Every milestone deserves recognition
5. **Inspiration**: Higher levels inspire others without superiority

### Messaging Tone
- Epic but grounded
- Inspiring but realistic  
- Personal but universal
- Challenging but supportive
- Mystical but practical
